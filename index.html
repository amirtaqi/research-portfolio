Patents & Inventions

Intellectual property spanning electrochemical water treatment, ocean-based CO₂ removal, and advanced energy & thermal-fluid systems.

I am an inventor or co-inventor on 3 patents / patent applications that are publicly visible and 6 additional filings in progress. Because several of these are still under review or confidential, the descriptions below focus on the technical themes rather than formal legal titles.

Electrochemical Water Treatment Platforms

Patent work on flow-through electrochemical reactors and granular activated carbon electrodes that generate reactive oxygen species in situ for the degradation of organic contaminants and emerging pollutants. These inventions tie directly to my multiscale modeling and experimental work on ROS-generating electrodes and reactor design.

Themes: electro-Fenton(-like) processes, porous electrode engineering, high-throughput flow reactors, and scalable architectures for decentralized water treatment.

Ocean-Based CO₂ Removal & Marine Electrochemistry

Inventions that integrate electrochemical ocean iron fertilization, alkalinity enhancement, and in some cases hydrogen co-production, enabling controlled nutrient release and quantifiable, durable marine CO₂ sequestration. These concepts are closely linked to my work on electrochemical ocean carbon removal reactors.

Themes: marine carbon dioxide removal (mCDR), electrochemical iron cycling, alkalinity management, and coupled MRV (monitoring, reporting, verification) frameworks.

Advanced Energy & Thermal-Fluid Systems

Patent activity in energy and thermal management, including hydrogen- and metal-based heat generation concepts and system-level designs that connect thermal-fluid science to batteries, electrochemical devices, and broader energy systems.

Themes: heat-generating materials and systems, thermal management strategies for electrochemical devices and reactors, and integration with battery and grid-scale applications.
